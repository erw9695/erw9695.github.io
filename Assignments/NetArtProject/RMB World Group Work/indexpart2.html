  • RMB City is a platform for experimental creative activities, Cao and her fellow artists who worked on this project like Uli Sigg used different physical and digital meidums to explore the border between virtual and physical existence. This platform at the time was very small and at the time foreign to most people as a concept, during the time it was active, Art Institutions could organize online gatherings and events in a virtual format, an early version of what we now know today as "The Metaverse."
